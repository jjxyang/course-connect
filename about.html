About Course Connect...


Why did you make this site?

This is a web project developed by Howe Cui and Jessie Yang for CS 194-32: Computing for Social Good!

We created this platform to help students find each other when they'd like help. Berkeley is a big campus with a lot of people. Sometimes you feel a little lost or you'd like someone to lend an ear. Sometimes you'd like to help out when you're able to. So, we're striving to foster a culture of collaboration, community, and support, enabling users with a tool that starts—but does not end!—at academic collaboration.
